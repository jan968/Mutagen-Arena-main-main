In a world where evolution is a weapon, how much of your humanity will you trade for victory? Fight, mutate, and climb the Elo ladder in a high-stakes battle arena where Level 99 is a legend—and every defeat at the top costs you more than just pride.

A text-based Battle Arena MMORPG built for competitive theory-crafters. Featuring a dynamic combat engine where stats drive your identity and RNG determines your evolution.

Adaptive Combat: 100% logic-driven battle playback with deep stat interactions (HP/STR/AGI).

The 99 Grind: A brutal, Diablo-inspired leveling curve. Fast to start, legendary to finish.

Unstable Mutations: 40+ unique combat traits. Draft your build, but choose wisely—some changes are permanent.

High-Stakes PvP: Integrated Elo rating system with XP-loss penalties for high-level defeats.
